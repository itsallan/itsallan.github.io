Kawogo

Last updated: April 2026

Kawogo is a personal video player app for iPhone.

Information we collect

Kawogo does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not track you or your activity.

Account information

If you choose to create an account, your name and email address are stored securely via Appwrite for authentication purposes only. This information is never shared with third parties.

Video content

Videos you play through Kawogo — whether from a pasted link or your Camera Roll — are played locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to any of your videos.

Camera Roll access

Kawogo requests access to your Photos library only to let you browse and play videos saved on your device. This data never leaves your device.

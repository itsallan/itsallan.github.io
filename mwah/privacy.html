Privacy Policy

We keep it simple because Mwah is simple.

Last updated March 2026
Also read our Terms of Service →

What is Mwah?

Mwah is a couples app. You tap a button to send a small signal — a "mwah" — to your partner. That is it. No feed, no chat, no social network. Just a little heartbeat between two people.

What we collect

Only what the app needs to work:

  • Your email and password, or your Apple ID if you use Sign in with Apple
  • A display name you choose yourself
  • Your tap activity — how many mwahs you send each day and when
  • Your anniversary date, if you choose to set one
  • The mood you attach to a mwah, if you pick one
  • The last time you opened the app, used to show your partner an online indicator
  • Photos you choose to add to your couple space memory wall

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other device data.

How we use your data

Exclusively to make the app work:

  • To send your mwahs to your partner in real time
  • To show your daily and weekly tap counts
  • To display your streak and anniversary
  • To keep your account secure

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with anyone except the services listed below that power the app.

Third party services

Mwah is built on Supabase, which handles authentication, database storage, and real-time messaging. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers. Read their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Photos you upload to the memory wall are stored in Supabase Storage. They are private to your couple — only you and your paired partner can view them.

Push notifications are delivered via OneSignal. Your device token is stored to enable notifications. Read their privacy policy at onesignal.com/privacy_policy.

If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple handles your authentication. No personal information from your Apple account is shared with us beyond your name and email, and only if you choose to share them.

What your partner sees

When you pair with someone, they can see:

  • Your display name
  • How many mwahs you have sent today and this week
  • The mood you attach to a mwah
  • Whether you have been online recently
  • Photos you have both added to the shared memory wall

They cannot see your email address, password, or any other account details.

Data deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. When you do, all of your data — your profile, tap history, and connection with your partner — is permanently deleted within 24 hours. This cannot be undone.

Children

Mwah is not intended for anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes

If we make meaningful changes to this policy we will update the date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at hi@mwah.app — we actually read them.
